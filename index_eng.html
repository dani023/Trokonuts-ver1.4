From the palm tree

to the palm of your hand

GOMUSA processes coconut by-products under the Tropinuts and Trokonuts brands at the Costa Rica plant. Coconut CREAM, coconut MILK is produced and packaged. In addition, sweet coconut FLAKES are produced. It offers capacity for PRIVATE BRANDS for export.

About Us

In 1984, a former employee of the local Coca-Cola bottling company, decided to invest his savings and start, as a family activity, the planting and sale of peanuts. Little by little, lines were added within the category such as salty, Mixed with cashew and confit (garapiñado).


The acceptance by its clients and consumers was so positive that in 2001 it decided to expand in the processing of coconut with lines of dehydration, canned coconut cream and milk and flakes under the brand name Tropinuts, Trokonuts or private brands for third parties.

The processing plant grows so much and so rapidly that it had to be transferred to a town in the Northeast of the country and closer to the coconut plantations and the ports of the Atlantic and Pacific. In addition to serving the domestic market, it has exported its surpluses to Mexico, Colombia, France and England. After another plant expansion, sufficient capacity is available to continue the domestic effort and focus on the international market more aggressively.


The company continues with its winning formula based on a lot of effort, loyal and trained human resources, continuous assurance of quality, integrity, responsibility, investment in equipment and technology so that with its advantageous price policy, customers perceive an extraordinary value in the products and services provided by PRODUCTOS ALIMENTICIOS GOMUSA.
